Meet My Pet Dog

Breed: Golden Retriever

Age: 3 years old

Personality: Playful and friendly

My pet dog is a golden retriever named Max. He is 3 years old and loves to play fetch and go for long walks. Max is very friendly and loves to meet new people. He is always wagging his tail and ready for a belly rub!

Daily Routine

  • Wake up and give a big stretch
  • Lick Mom and Dad
  • Move to the couch
  • Wait by door until mom leaves
  • Make mom feel bad about leaving
  • Sleep
  • Wait for Maddie to take me on the walk
  • Go for a walk
  • Get photo taken by Maddie
  • Take a dump
  • Go back to sleep
  • Wake up when you hear keys jingle to unlock the door
  • Lick Mom and Dad until bed time
  • Hog the bed
  • Repeat

Health and Wellness

Norman is in good health and has all of his vaccinations up to date. He eats a high-quality dog food and gets plenty of exercise. However Norman has a Coefficient of Inbreeding of 45% which the vet says means his parents were siblings.

Fun Facts and Anecdotes

  • Max loves to dig holes in the backyard and bury his toys.
  • He can be a bit of a messy eater and is always looking for scraps from the kitchen.
  • Max has a great sense of smell and can always find the best spot to roll in the grass.
